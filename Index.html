About Contractorology

At Contractorology, we believe in delivering quality and reliability. The name Contractorology embodies the science and art of contracting, bringing together expertise, precision, and dedication to every project we undertake.

Brandon, the owner, started his career with the town's parks and recreation department, managing parks and facilities with a dedicated crew. He was later promoted to lead the construction of a $10.5 million park, featuring a disc golf course, trails, a large lake, and a beautiful waterfall.

Brandon then launched his own handyman business, Lightning Handyman. Seeking further growth, he joined a company building indoor trampoline parks worldwide, progressing from an installer to a superintendent managing multi-million dollar projects. Brandon eventually led his own team, installing colorful epoxy floors across the country.

Returning to entrepreneurship, Brandon founded Contractorology, a general contracting company that has assisted in building Amazon warehouses in the PNW and completed various other significant projects.
